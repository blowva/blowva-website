How it works

Four steps to blowing up

From signup to your first viral post in under 10 minutes.

1

Create your account & pick your niche

Sign up in seconds. Tell Blowva your niche — fashion, food, comedy, music, education, anything — and the AI starts learning your space immediately.

2

Link your TikTok, Instagram & Facebook

Connect all your accounts with one click. Blowva reads your existing content and audience data to build a personalised growth strategy for you.

3

Upload your video — Blowva does the rest

Drop your video into Blowva. AI writes your caption and hashtags, picks the perfect posting time, and publishes it across all your platforms automatically.

4

Get smarter content suggestions over time

The longer you use Blowva, the smarter it gets. It tracks what works, spots trends early, and tells you exactly what to create next — and when.
