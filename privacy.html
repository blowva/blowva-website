Privacy Policy

How Blowva collects, uses, and protects your information

Last updated: April 1, 2026
Your privacy matters to us. Blowva is built for Nigerian creators and we take your data seriously. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

1. Who we are

Blowva ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered social media growth platform built for Nigerian and African content creators. Our website is located at blowva.com. We help creators automatically generate captions, hashtags, and publish content across TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: hello@blowva.com

2. Information we collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Blowva:

  • Account information — Your full name, email address, and content niche when you register
  • Social media account data — When you connect your TikTok, Instagram, or Facebook accounts, we access your profile information, follower counts, and post performance data
  • Content you upload — Videos you upload to Blowva for processing and publishing
  • Usage data — How you interact with our platform, which features you use, and when you use them
  • Device information — Your browser type, operating system, and IP address

3. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your Blowva account
  • Generate AI-powered captions and hashtags for your videos
  • Publish your content to your connected social media accounts
  • Analyse your content performance and provide growth suggestions
  • Send you important updates about your account and our service
  • Improve our AI models and platform features
  • Provide customer support

4. Social media permissions

When you connect your social media accounts to Blowva, we request only the permissions we need to provide our service:

  • Instagram — We request permission to read your profile, view your media, and publish content on your behalf through the Instagram Graph API
  • Facebook — We request permission to manage your pages and publish content through the Facebook Graph API
  • TikTok — We request permission to upload and publish videos to your TikTok account through the TikTok Content Posting API

You can revoke these permissions at any time from your social media account settings or from your Blowva account settings page.

5. How we store your data

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which uses industry-standard encryption and security practices. Your videos are stored temporarily for processing and publishing, then deleted from our servers within 30 days unless you choose to keep them in your Blowva library.

Our servers are located in the European Union (Ireland). By using Blowva, you consent to your data being processed in this location.

6. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information to anyone. We only share your data with:

  • Social media platforms — When you instruct us to publish content on your behalf
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — Your video descriptions are sent to Anthropic's API to generate captions. Anthropic does not store or use this data to train their models
  • Service providers — Companies that help us operate our platform (hosting, analytics) under strict data protection agreements
  • Legal requirements — If required by law or to protect our rights

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Disconnect your social media accounts at any time
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@blowva.com

8. Cookies

Blowva uses essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising cookies or track you across other websites. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect how Blowva works.

9. Children's privacy

Blowva is not intended for anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at hello@blowva.com

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you by email or through a notice on our website. Your continued use of Blowva after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests about your privacy, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@blowva.com
  • Website: blowva.com
